How We Work

From your first call to final installation – 5 simple steps, zero hassle for you.

Step-by-Step Process

Give us space. We handle everything from measurement to final installation.

Step 1

Free Site Visit

Call us or send a WhatsApp message. We'll schedule a free site visit anywhere in Ahmedabad. Our team comes to your location to understand your requirements, assess the site conditions, and discuss options.

What we check: Wall condition, mounting points, accessibility, lighting, and visibility angles.

Step 2

Precise Measurement

Using professional tools, we take exact measurements of the installation area. Accurate measurements ensure perfect fit – no gaps, no overlaps, no wastage.

What you get: Exact dimensions report with photos of the site for reference.

Step 3

Design Suggestion

Based on measurements and your brand guidelines, we suggest the best layout, colors, and design approach. Even if you don't have a design ready, we'll help you create one.

Includes: Layout mockup, color recommendations, size optimization, and print-ready file preparation.

Step 4

HD Printing

Once you approve the design, we print on industrial-grade large format printers. HD quality output with vibrant, UV-resistant inks that last for months outdoors.

Quality check: Color accuracy, resolution verification, and material inspection before delivery.

Step 5

Professional Installation

Our skilled team handles complete installation – drilling, fixing, hanging, and finishing. We ensure the print is level, secure, and looks perfect from every angle.

Includes: All mounting hardware, drilling, frame fixing, tension adjustment, and site cleanup.
